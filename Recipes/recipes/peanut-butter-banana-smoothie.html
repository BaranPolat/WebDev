Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

This peanut butter banana smoothie is so refreshing, and it's sweet and tasty. Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • Â½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender
  2. blend until smooth, about 30 seconds
